Encircle

Mobile native app built with React Native

The Project

This was for my senior project and we decided to work with Encircle, a non-profit that helps LGBTQ teen with therapy, resources, events, and even housing. They wanted an app for their users where they could browse the store, look up events based on location, find resources, and get connected with their programs.

For this project I was on a team with 2 designers and 2 other developers. We used React Native to build the app over the course of two semesters. I primarily worked on the Shop page, which pulled items from their Shopify API. The other bulk of my work was styling every component.

What I learned:

  • React Native
  • Working with XCode/Android Simulator
  • Many different API's and response formats
  • Design/wireframe process/UX
  • That I love to work on a team
