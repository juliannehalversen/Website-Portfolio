About Me

My name is Julianne Lee and I'm a web and app developer. I'm most passionate about frontend development but eventually I would love to work as a full stack engineer. One of the many reasons I love what I do is the mix of technical and creative skills it requires. It's the perfect mixture of the two for me and I feel so rewarded creating something that looks beautiful but will also serve as a better user experience and drive business goals. I love the constant change and learning required for this field and am always looking for ways to improve.

Besides coding, you can find me rock climbing, backpacking, playing the cello, or gaming. I also love finding a new online course to help me stay up to date with industry trends.
